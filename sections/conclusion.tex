\section{Conclusion}\label{sec:conclusion}

Even though the list of objects that a real object-oriented system may need
  could be huge, we believe that it is possible to have a reasonably small
  foundation beneath any large system.
We just presented such a foundation.
Future works may try to validate our assumption empirically.
